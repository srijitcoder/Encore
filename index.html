EnCore (Enthusiastic Computer Research Experts) is an
IT professional's CS club of NGF School, saket. Started in 2015 by Mr. Prakash Dwivedi
and his peers to encourage young youthsparks in the field of IT. The club focus on core
development of a child's IT talent by focusing on his/her core elements.
We host seminars, symposium, hackathon, quizzes, fest etc.
We like to provide students with best whether it's coding or tech. Our team love to do coding, designing,
app development, photography, flash, imaging, A/V mixers, web designing, chat with techies and update ourself with latest techs.
